Cricket is a team sport played with a bat and ball between two teams of eleven players each. The game is played on an oval-shaped field with a pitch at the center. The objective is to score runs by hitting the ball and running between the wickets or by hitting boundaries (four or six runs). One team bats while the other bowls and fields, trying to dismiss the batsmen by various means such as bowling them out, catching, or running them out. Matches can last from a few hours (Twenty20) to several days (Test cricket). Cricket is popular worldwide, especially in countries like India, England, Australia, and Pakistan.
